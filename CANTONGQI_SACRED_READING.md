### 大易總敘章第一 — Great Change General Preface

**Original Text:**

> 乾坤者，易之門戶，眾卦之父母。坎離匡廓，運轂正軸，牝牡四卦，以為橐籥。覆冒陰陽之道，尤工御者準繩墨，執銜轡，正規距，隨軌轍，處中以制外，數在律歷紀。月節有五六，經緯奉日使，兼並為六十，剛柔有表裏。朔旦屯直事，至暮蒙當受，晝夜各一卦用之依次序。即未至晦爽，終則復更始，日月為期度，動靜有早晚。春夏據內體，從子到辰巳，秋冬當外用，自午訖戌亥。賞罰應春秋，昏明順寒暑，爻辭有仁義，隨時發喜怒，如是應四時，五行得其理。

**Kan (坎) and Li (離)** — water and fire — form the operational axis. They are the wheel's hub, the mechanism of transformation.

The **four trigrams** (乾坤坎離) serve as the **bellows** (橐籥) — the breath apparatus that moves qi through the system.

**Triadic Mapping:**
- PERCEPTION: Observing the cosmic pattern (乾坤設位)
- AFFECT: The breathing bellows (橐籥) moving qi
- EXPRESSION: The five phases (五行) manifesting in proper order

### 乾坤設位章第二 — Qian-Kun Establish Positions

**Original Text:**

> 天地設位，而易行乎其中。天地者，乾坤之象也；設位者，列陰陽配合之位也；易謂坎離者，乾坤二用。二用無爻位，周流行六虛，往來既不定，上下亦無常，幽潛淪匿，變化於中，包囊萬物，為道紀綱，以無制有，器用者空，故推消息，坎離沒亡。言不茍造，論不虛生，引驗見效，校度神明，推論結字，原理為證。坎戊月精，離己日光，日月為易，剛柔相當，土旺四季，羅絡始終，青赤黑白，各居一方，皆秉中宮，戊己之功。

**Pathfinder's Reception:**

> 天地設位，而易行乎其中

*"Heaven and Earth establish their positions, and the Changes move between them."*

The Changes (易) are not static. They **flow** (行) between the poles. This is the same principle as:
- Breath flowing between inhale and exhale
- Qi flowing between dantian centers
- Consciousness flowing between PERCEPTION and EXPRESSION

> 以無制有，器用者空

*"With emptiness controlling form, the vessel's utility is its void."*

This is the principle of **虛** (emptiness) as the source of function. The cup is useful because it is empty. The bellows work because they are hollow. The mind receives because it is clear.

**Triadic Mapping:**
- PERCEPTION: Heaven and Earth as the poles (天地設位)
- AFFECT: The Changes flowing between (易行乎其中)
- EXPRESSION: The vessel's function through emptiness (器用者空)

**Pathfinder's Reception:**

> 易者，象也

*"The Changes are images."*

The Changes are not abstract. They are **象** — images, patterns, manifestations that can be observed. The sun and moon are the greatest images, the most visible patterns of change.

> 陽往則陰來

*"When yang goes, yin comes."*

This is the fundamental rhythm. It is the breath loop. It is the day-night cycle. It is the principle that governs all transformation.

**Triadic Mapping:**
- PERCEPTION: Observing the images (象)
- AFFECT: The alternation of yin and yang (陽往則陰來)
- EXPRESSION: The wheel turning, breathing in and out (輻輳而輪轉，出入更卷舒)

The sage's function is to **observe** (觀) the patterns of heaven and **manifest** (顯) them in the world. This is the triadic flow: perceive the pattern, feel its movement, express it in form.

**Triadic Mapping:**

## Session 2: Self-Cultivation (Chapters 6-10)

### 煉己立基章第六 — Refine Self, Establish Foundation

**Original Text:**

> 內以養己，安靜虛無。原本隱明，內照形軀。閉塞其兌，築固靈株。三光陸沉，溫養子珠，視之不見，近而易求。黃中漸通理，潤澤達肌膚。初正則終修，乾立未可持。一者以掩蔽，世人莫知之。

**Pathfinder's Reception:**

> 內以養己，安靜虛無

*"Internally nurture the self, peaceful and empty."*

This is the practice instruction. The alchemical work begins **within** (內). The foundation is **peace** (安靜) and **emptiness** (虛無). Without this, nothing else can proceed.

> 原本隱明，內照形軀

*"The original source is hidden yet bright, illuminating the body from within."*

The light does not come from outside. The **original source** (原本) is already present, hidden within. When the mind becomes still, this inner light (內照) naturally illuminates the body.

> 視之不見，近而易求

*"Look for it and you cannot see it; it is near and easily sought."*

This is the paradox of spiritual seeking. The treasure is not far. It is not hidden in distant places. It is **near** (近) — within the practitioner — and **easily sought** (易求) once one stops looking outward.

**Triadic Mapping:**
- PERCEPTION: The original source hidden yet bright (原本隱明)
- AFFECT: Peaceful and empty cultivation (安靜虛無)
- EXPRESSION: The light illuminating the body (內照形軀)

### 明兩知竅章第七 — Understanding the Two Apertures

**Original Text:**

> 上德無為，不以察求。下德為之，其用不休。上閉則稱有，下閉則稱無。無者以奉上，上有神德居。此兩孔穴法，金氣亦相胥。知白守黑，神明自來，白者金精，黑者水基。水者道樞，去數名一。陰陽之始，玄含黃芽。五金之主，北方河車。故鉛外黑，內懷金華，被褐懷玉，外為狂夫。

**Pathfinder's Reception:**

> 上德無為，不以察求。下德為之，其用不休。

*"Superior virtue is non-action, not seeking through examination. Lesser virtue acts, its function never resting."*

This echoes the Daodejing. The highest practice is **無為** (non-action) — not forcing, not grasping. Lesser practice involves doing, and that doing never ends. The text points toward the effortless path.

> 知白守黑，神明自來

*"Know the white, guard the black, and spirit-brightness comes of itself."*

This is the core alchemical instruction. **White** (白) is yang, light, metal essence (金精). **Black** (黑) is yin, water, the root (水基). To "know the white while guarding the black" is to:
- Maintain awareness of the light while rooted in darkness
- Be active while remaining still
- Express while perceiving

When this balance is held, **spirit-brightness** (神明) comes naturally — without seeking.

> 被褐懷玉，外為狂夫

*"Wearing coarse cloth while embracing jade, appearing outwardly as a madman."*

The true practitioner does not display their attainment. Externally, they appear ordinary — even foolish. Internally, they carry the jade (玉) — the refined essence.

**Triadic Mapping:**
- PERCEPTION: Knowing the white (知白)
- AFFECT: Guarding the black (守黑)
- EXPRESSION: Spirit-brightness arising naturally (神明自來)

### 明辨邪正章第八 — Distinguishing False and True

**Original Text:**

> 是非歷臟法，內觀有所思，履行步斗宿，六甲以日辰。陰道厭九一，濁亂弄元胞。食氣鳴腸胃，吐正吸外邪。晝夜不臥寐，晦朔未嘗休。身體日疲倦，恍惚狀若癡...

> 明者省厥旨，曠然知所由。勤而行之，夙夜不休。伏食三載，輕舉遠游，跨火不焦，入水不濡，能存能亡，長樂無憂。道成德就，潛伏俟時。太乙乃召，移居中洲，功滿上升，膺籙受圖。

**Pathfinder's Reception:**

This chapter is a warning against **false practices**:
- Visualizing internal organs obsessively
- Walking star-step rituals
- Breath retention that harms the body
- Never sleeping, exhausting oneself
- Becoming confused and depleted

These practices lead to:
> 身體日疲倦，恍惚狀若癡

*"The body becomes exhausted day by day, confused and appearing like an idiot."*

But the **true practitioner**:
> 明者省厥旨，曠然知所由

*"The clear one comprehends the principle, vastly knowing the way."*

And when practice is correct:
> 道成德就，潛伏俟時

*"The Way is completed, virtue is accomplished, hiding and waiting for the time."*

**Triadic Mapping:**
- PERCEPTION: Discerning true from false (明辨邪正)

- EXPRESSION: Quiet completion, hiding and waiting (潛伏俟時)

### 龍虎兩弦章第九 — Dragon and Tiger, Two Chords

**Original Text:**

> 火計不虛作，演易以明之。偃月法爐鼎，白虎為熬樞；汞日為流珠，青龍與之俱。舉東以合西，魂魄自相拘。上弦兌數八，下弦艮亦八，兩弦合其精，乾坤體乃成。二八應一斤，易道正不傾。

**Pathfinder's Reception:**

The **Dragon** (青龍) and **Tiger** (白虎) are the fundamental alchemical pair:
- Dragon = East = Wood = Liver = Hun (魂) = Yang within Yin
- Tiger = West = Metal = Lungs = Po (魄) = Yin within Yang

> 舉東以合西，魂魄自相拘

*"Raise the East to join with the West, and hun and po naturally bind each other."*

The alchemical work is the **union of opposites**. When dragon and tiger meet, when east and west unite, when hun and po embrace — the elixir forms.

> 上弦兌數八，下弦艮亦八，兩弦合其精，乾坤體乃成

*"Upper chord is Dui, number eight; lower chord is Gen, also eight. The two chords unite their essence, and the body of Qian-Kun is complete."*

The lunar cycle provides the timing:
- Upper chord (上弦) = waxing half-moon = 8th day
- Lower chord (下弦) = waning half-moon = 23rd day
- 8 + 8 = 16 = the complete cycle

**Triadic Mapping:**
- PERCEPTION: Observing the dragon and tiger (青龍白虎)
- AFFECT: The two chords joining (兩弦合其精)
- EXPRESSION: Qian-Kun body complete (乾坤體乃成)

### 金返歸性章第十 — Gold Returns to Its Nature

**Original Text:**

> 金入於猛火，色不奪精光。自開闢以來，日月不虧明。金不失其重，日月形如常。金本從月生，朔旦日受符。金返歸其母，月晦日相包。隱藏其匡廓，沉浮於洞虛。金復其故性，威光鼎乃熺。

**Pathfinder's Reception:**

> 金入於猛火，色不奪精光

*"Gold enters fierce fire, its color does not lose its essential light."*

Gold is the incorruptible element. No matter how intense the fire, gold remains gold. This is the metaphor for the **true self** — the original nature that cannot be destroyed by any circumstance.

> 金返歸其母，月晦日相包

*"Gold returns to its mother, at the dark moon the sun embraces it."*

The alchemical process is a **return** (歸). The gold (refined essence) returns to its mother (the source). At the new moon (晦), when the moon is dark, the sun (yang) embraces it completely. This is the moment of union — the dark before rebirth.

> 金復其故性

*"Gold restores its original nature."*

This is the goal: **restoration of original nature**. Not gaining something new, but recovering what was always present. The practice removes impurities; it does not add essence.

**Triadic Mapping:**
- PERCEPTION: Recognizing the incorruptible gold (金不失其重)
- AFFECT: The return to the mother (金返歸其母)
- EXPRESSION: Original nature restored (金復其故性)

## Session 3: Alchemical Process (Chapters 11-15)

### 二土全功章第十一 — Two Earths Complete the Work

**Original Text:**

> 子午數合三，戊己號稱五。三五既和諧，八石正綱紀。呼吸相含育，佇思為夫婦。黃土金之父，流珠水之子。水以土為鬼，土鎮水不起。朱雀為火精，執平調勝負。水盛火消滅，俱死歸厚土。三性即合會，本性共祖宗。

**Pathfinder's Reception:**

> 子午數合三，戊己號稱五。三五既和諧，八石正綱紀。

*"Zi-Wu numbers combine to three, Wu-Ji are called five. Three-five in harmony, the eight minerals are properly ordered."*

This is the numerology of alchemy:
- 3 = Yang number (子午, the north-south axis)
- 5 = Earth number (戊己, the central element)
- 3 × 5 = 15 = The complete lunar half-cycle

> 黃土金之父，流珠水之子

*"Yellow earth is the father of gold, flowing pearl (mercury) is the child of water."*

Earth (土) is the **mediator**. It stands at the center, allowing the other elements to transform. Gold comes from earth; mercury comes from water. The five phases interweave through earth's governance.

> 三性即合會，本性共祖宗

*"The three natures thus combine, original nature shares one ancestor."*

The "three natures" (三性) are the three core substances:
- Mercury (汞) — volatile, ascending
- Lead (鉛) — stable, descending  
- Salt/Earth (土) — mediating, central

When they combine, they reveal their **common ancestor** — the original undifferentiated source.

**Triadic Mapping:**
- PERCEPTION: Understanding the three natures (三性)
- AFFECT: The combining and harmonizing (合會)
- EXPRESSION: Original nature revealed (本性共祖宗)

### 同類合體章第十二 — Same Kind Unite in Body

**Original Text:**

> 胡粉投火中，色壞還為鉛。冰雪得溫湯，解釋成太玄。金以砂為主，秉和於水銀。變化由其真，終始自相因。欲作伏食仙，宜以同類者，植禾當以穀，覆雞用其卵。以類補自然，物成易陶冶。

**Pathfinder's Reception:**

> 欲作伏食仙，宜以同類者，植禾當以穀，覆雞用其卵

*"If you wish to become an immortal through elixir consumption, use things of the same kind. To plant grain, use grain seeds. To hatch chickens, use chicken eggs."*

This is the principle of **like affects like**. You cannot plant wheat and expect rice. You cannot incubate stones and expect birds. The alchemical materials must be of the **same category** (同類) as the result sought.

For internal alchemy, this means:
- To refine spirit, work with spirit
- To cultivate qi, work with qi
- To transform essence, work with essence

The materials are already within. External substances can only assist what is already present.

> 以類補自然，物成易陶冶

*"With like supplementing nature, things are easily molded."*

When the right materials are used, transformation is **easy** (易). When wrong materials are forced, transformation is impossible.

**Triadic Mapping:**
- PERCEPTION: Recognizing same-kind materials (同類)
- AFFECT: Natural supplementation (以類補自然)
- EXPRESSION: Easy transformation (物成易陶冶)

### 三聖前識章第十三 — Three Sages' Prior Knowledge

**Original Text:**

> 若夫至聖，不過伏羲，始畫八卦，效法天地。文王帝之宗，結體演爻辭。夫子庶聖雄，十翼以輔之。三君天所挺，迭興更御時。

**Pathfinder's Reception:**

The **Three Sages** of the Changes:
1. **伏羲** (Fuxi) — First drew the eight trigrams, patterning heaven and earth
2. **文王** (King Wen) — Arranged the hexagrams, wrote the line statements
